About Us



Fastrack is an Indian fashion accessory brand, launched in 1998 as a sub-brand of Titan Watches. In 2005, Fastrack was spun off as an independent brand targeting the urban youth and growing fashion industry in India.[1] Fastrack began opening retail stores throughout the country. The first store was opened in early 2009.[2][3] Fastrack Entered the Affordable Smart Segment with the Launch of Reflex Beat+ in 2023[4] Fastrack now has presence across multiple categories, like eyewear, backpacks, watches and wearables.
